Chocolate Cake Recipe

Below is a short example of how our chocolate cake is prepared.

Ingredients

  • Flour - 2 cups
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Cocoa Powder - 1/2 cup
  • Eggs - 2 pcs
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Butter - 1/2 cup

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, cocoa powder, and sugar.
  2. Add eggs, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
